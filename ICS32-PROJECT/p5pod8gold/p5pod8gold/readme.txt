Name: Spot Game v2.0

Spot Game v2.0 is a brand new version. In order to win, you have to exert yourself. Trust me, your mouse speed really matters.
In level one, there will be 10 spots, and you need to click on each of them to pass. Then, in level two, there will be 15 spots, and they will be moving much faster than in level one. You also need to click each spots to make them disappear. Enjoy gaming and good luck!
