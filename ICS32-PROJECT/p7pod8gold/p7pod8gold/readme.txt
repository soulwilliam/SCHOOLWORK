Program Name: Connect4 V3.0 - "Black and White"

Explanation: Our program renders a connect four game. The game consist of two players going against one another. Like any connect four game, in order to win, one must have their drops aligned 4 in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Moreover, you get to choose how large you want your game dimensions to be; rows of 6 to 14, and columns of 6 and also up to 14. To start the game, you may run the controller file and press <ENTER> in the first window.

Difficulty: 8

Third Party Libraries: tkinter
