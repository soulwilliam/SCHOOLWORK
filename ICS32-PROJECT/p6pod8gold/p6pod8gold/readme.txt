Program Name: Connect4 V2.0 - "Trump vs Putin"

Explanation: Our program renders a connect four game. The game consist of two players going against one another. Like any connect four game, in order to win, one must have their drops aligned 4 in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Moreover, you get to choose how large you want your game dimensions to be; rows of 6 to 10, and columns of 6 and also up to 10. To start the game, you may run the view_controller.py file.

Difficulty: 8

Third Party Libraries: pygame
